📋 JOB DESCRIPTION AND GRANDMA'S CARE

Complete Caregiver Responsibilities & Daily Duties Guide

Long Term Position - 2 Years On Going

Position Overview

Essential details about the caregiver position and employment terms.

Employment Details

Position Type Full-Time Caregiver
Duration Long Term - 2 Years On Going +++++
Primary Responsibility Be with mum at all times
Location Residential Care

Key Responsibilities

Core duties and responsibilities for ensuring grandma's wellbeing and comfort.

1. Mum's Wellbeing Management

  • Washing and personal hygiene assistance
  • Cleaning and maintaining living spaces
  • Cooking and meal preparation
  • Ensuring Mum eats on time
  • Administering medication as prescribed
  • Providing any other help mum needs

2. Escort and Transportation

  • Escort mum whenever she's going out
  • Accompany to social occasions and events
  • Attend medical appointments with mum
  • Provide feedback on appointments to family

3. Medication Management

  • Help grandma with her medication schedule
  • Ensure she has taken medication on time
  • Refill medication box for the week
  • Monitor and track medication adherence

4. Personal Care Assistance

  • Help grandma with dressing up when going out
  • Select appropriate and comfortable clothing
  • Ensure dignity and comfort in appearance

Daily Duties Checklist

Complete guide to daily tasks and responsibilities for maintaining a clean, safe, and comfortable environment.

Bathroom & Hygiene

  • Clean mum's bathroom thoroughly
  • Clean sink and toilet
  • Ensure hygiene supplies are stocked

Bedroom Maintenance

  • Make the bed every morning
  • Sweep the bedroom floor
  • Dust side table and headboard
  • Ensure mum's room is clean and tidy

Compound & Outdoor

  • Sweep the compound regularly
  • Clean around flowers and mango tree
  • Remove fallen leaves

Pet Care

  • Clean the dog's bowls daily
  • Provide clean water daily
  • Feed dog twice daily

Kitchen Duties HIGH PRIORITY

  • Wash plates and saucepans daily
  • Empty kitchen bin every night
  • Sweep kitchen floor
  • Clean all surfaces
  • Mop floor regularly

Security & Safety CRITICAL

  • Lock kitchen back door when not in use
  • Ensure main gate is closed with padlock
  • Monitor visitor entry and exit
  • Maintain security at all times

Laundry Services

  • Wash mum's clothing regularly
  • Wash towels and bed linen
  • Wash when mum requests
  • Don't let laundry pile up
  • Iron clothes when needed

Weekly Schedule

Specific tasks assigned to particular days for systematic completion.

Day Assigned Tasks
MONDAY
  • • Change mum's bed
  • • Wash towels
  • • Wash bed sheets and other linens
WEDNESDAY
  • • Clean windows
  • • Mop all floors
THURSDAY
  • • Change mum's bed
  • • Wash towels
  • • Wash bed sheets and other linens

Important Reminders

  • Kitchen must be clean and tidy at all times
  • Always ensure security doors and gates are locked
  • Medication must be taken on time - no exceptions
  • Don't let laundry pile up - wash regularly
  • Provide feedback on all appointments and outings
  • Be with mum at all times as required
