GUIDE TO DAILY DUTIES AND OTHER STUFF TO DO

Detailed daily tasks and responsibilities to maintain a clean, safe, and comfortable environment.

1. Bathroom Cleaning

  • Please clean mum bathroom, sink and toilet

2. Bedroom Maintenance

  • Make the bed and sweep the floor
  • And dust side table and head board bed
  • In general please make sure mum's room is clean

3. Compound Cleaning

  • Sweep the compound espeacially around the flowers and mango tree
  • When leaves are fallen

4. Pet Care

  • Please clean the dog's bowls daily
  • Give dog clean water and food daily
  • Food twice

5. Kitchen Duties

  • Please wash plates and saucepans daily
  • Empty kitchen bin every night
  • Sweep the floor and clean surfaces
  • Please mop the floor regularly
* Kitchen must be clean & tidy

6. Security & Safety

  • Please make sure kitchen back door is locked always, when not in kitchen for safety
  • And also make sure main gate is close with padlock
  • When visitors come in and leaves

7. Laundry

  • Please wash mum's clothing, towels, bed linen regularly and when she wants you to do it
  • Please don't pile it
  • And please iron when needs to

Weekly Schedule

Specific tasks assigned to particular days of the week for systematic completion.

MONDAY & THURSDAY

  • Change mum's bed
  • Wash towels and bed sheets & others

WEDNESDAY

  • Clean windows
  • Mop the floors

📊 Daily Task Completion

Consistent completion of all daily duties ensures grandma's comfort, safety, and wellbeing. Track your progress and maintain the highest standards of care.

Your dedication makes a difference in providing quality care and a comfortable home environment.
