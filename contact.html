Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions from our users.

FAQ 01

How do I access my lessons?

All lessons are available directly through this website. Simply navigate to the relevant class and subject from the home page. No login is required for basic access.

FAQ 02

Is my progress saved automatically?

Yes! Your quiz scores, lesson progress, and dashboard data are automatically saved to your browser's local storage. Make sure not to clear your browser data if you want to keep your records.

FAQ 03

Can I switch between student and instructor roles?

Absolutely. Click the settings icon in the navigation bar and toggle between "Student" and "Instructor" modes. Your preference is saved for future visits.

FAQ 04

How do I share my quiz results?

After completing a quiz, click the "Share via WhatsApp" button. A formatted report will be generated and you can send it directly to your teacher, parent, or study group.

FAQ 05

Is the content aligned with NaCCA?

Yes, all our educational content is carefully aligned with the Ghana National Council for Curriculum and Assessment (NaCCA) standards-based curriculum for basic schools.

FAQ 06

Does it work offline?

Once loaded, most features work without an internet connection since content is stored locally. However, initial loading and some features require connectivity.
